About MyCAT

Having accurate church service attendee is very important when it comes to tracking a church or congregation growth on a weekly programs, and doing this has never been easier. Our app is designed to help you manage attendance efficiently and accurately, making it easier for your church to stay connected with its members.

Key Features of MyCAT

Easy Attendance Tracking: Quickly log attendance for Sunday services and other weekly programs with a user-friendly interface.


Quick Attendance Adjustments: Update attendance records quickly and easily.


Comprehensive Reports: Generate detailed reports to understand attendance trends and engage with your congregation.


Secure and Reliable: Your data is safe with us, ensuring privacy and reliability every step of the way.


Customizable: Tailor the app to meet the specific needs of your church community.
